Our Origin

The Lumina Story

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Elias Thorne, Ethereal Guardian emerged from a simple realization: that the world's most vulnerable ecosystems were being lost not through lack of care, but through a lack of focused, transparent stewardship. Our history is a journey of turning quiet intentions into global resonance.

Lumina was our first project—a restored coastal wetland that became the blueprint for our founding principles: Radical Transparency, Adaptive Resilience, and Ecosystem Sovereignty. We don't just protect land; we empower the silent rhythms of nature to heal themselves under our watchful guidance.
